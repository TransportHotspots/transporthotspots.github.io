Transport Hotspots is an enhanced route planning program different to Tripview. Our application make use of the open data supplied by Transport NSW and on top of that, crowd sourced data which is obtain from user of our application who will have the ability to rate each train stations base on the "heat" rating of the crowd.

  •   Very high density of people at the platform.
  •   Moderate density at the platform.
  •   Low density, easy to move around the platform.
